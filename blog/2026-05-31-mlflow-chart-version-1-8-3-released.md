# Mlflow Chart Version 1.8.3 Released!

We are excited to share the release of the **mlflow Helm chart version 1.8.3** on May 31, 2026, featuring **app version 3.12.0**. This update introduces a range of new features, enhancements, and community-driven improvements designed to streamline your deployment of mlflow on Kubernetes.

## What's New in Mlflow 1.8.3 Chart

This release includes updates to both the Helm chart (version 1.8.3) and the underlying application (version 3.12.0). Here's a summary of the key changes:

- **Fixed**: Liveness and readiness probes now consistently use /health, regardless of staticPrefix.
    - [GitHub Issue](https://github.com/community-charts/helm-charts/issues/290)

To explore the complete list of changes, please visit the [release notes](https://github.com/community-charts/helm-charts/releases/tag/mlflow-1.8.3) on GitHub.
