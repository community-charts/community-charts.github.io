# Cloudflared Chart Version 2.2.11 Released!

We are excited to share the launch of the **Cloudflared Helm chart version 2.2.11** on July 9, 2026, featuring **app version 2026.7.0**. This update introduces a variety of new features, enhancements, and improvements inspired by our vibrant community, all designed to simplify your deployment of Cloudflared on Kubernetes.

## What's New in Cloudflared 2.2.11 Chart

This release encompasses updates for both the Helm chart (version 2.2.11) and the underlying application (version 2026.7.0). Here’s a brief overview of what’s included in this version:

- **Changed**: Updated the Cloudflare/cloudflared image version to 2026.7.0
    - [Upstream Project](https://hub.docker.com/r/cloudflare/cloudflared)

To explore the complete list of changes, check out the [release notes](https://github.com/community-charts/helm-charts/releases/tag/cloudflared-2.2.11) on GitHub.
