# n8n chart version 1.20.0 released!

We are excited to share the launch of the **n8n Helm chart version 1.20.0** on June 7, 2026, featuring **app version 2.23.4**. This latest update introduces a variety of new features, enhancements, and community-driven improvements designed to streamline your experience with deploying n8n on Kubernetes.

## What's New in the n8n 1.20.0 Chart

This release encompasses updates to both the Helm chart (version 1.20.0) and the underlying application (version 2.23.4). Here’s a quick look at the key changes:

- **Changed**: Deprecated the `license.autoNenew` fields in favor of `license.autoRenew`, while maintaining backward compatibility.
    - [GitHub Issue](https://github.com/community-charts/helm-charts/issues/294)

For a detailed list of changes, feel free to check out the [release notes](https://github.com/community-charts/helm-charts/releases/tag/n8n-1.20.0) on GitHub.
