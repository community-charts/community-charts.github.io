# n8n chart version 1.22.1 released!

We are excited to share the release of the **n8n Helm chart version 1.22.1** on June 10, 2026, featuring **app version 2.25.6**! This update introduces a variety of new features, enhancements, and community-driven improvements designed to streamline your n8n deployment on Kubernetes.

## What's New in n8n 1.22.1 chart

With this release, you will find significant updates to both the Helm chart (version 1.22.1) and the underlying application (version 2.25.6). Here’s a detailed look at the changes:

- **Changed**: Updated the n8nio/n8n image version to 2.25.6
    - [Upstream Project](https://github.com/n8n-io/n8n)
- **Changed**: Updated dependency postgresql from 18.7.2 to 18.7.3
    - [ArtifactHub](https://artifacthub.io/packages/helm/bitnami/postgresql)

For a complete list of changes, be sure to check the [release notes](https://github.com/community-charts/helm-charts/releases/tag/n8n-1.22.1) on GitHub.
