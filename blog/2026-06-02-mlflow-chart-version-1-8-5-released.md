# MLflow Chart Version 1.8.5 Released!

We are excited to share the launch of the **MLflow Helm chart version 1.8.5** on June 2, 2026, featuring **app version 3.13.0**. This update brings a wealth of new features, enhancements, and community-driven improvements designed to streamline your MLflow deployment on Kubernetes.

## What's New in MLflow 1.8.5 Chart

This release includes updates to both the Helm chart (version 1.8.5) and the underlying application (version 3.13.0). Here are some of the key changes:

- **Fixed**: Resolved broken screenshot URL(s) in the MLflow chart.

For a comprehensive list of changes, you can view the [release notes](https://github.com/community-charts/helm-charts/releases/tag/mlflow-1.8.5) on GitHub.
