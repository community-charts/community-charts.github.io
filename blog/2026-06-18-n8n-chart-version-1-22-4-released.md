# n8n chart version 1.22.4 released!

We are excited to share the launch of the **n8n Helm chart version 1.22.4** on June 18, 2026, featuring **app version 2.26.6**. This significant update introduces fresh features, enhancements, and community-driven improvements, all aimed at making the deployment of n8n on Kubernetes smoother and more efficient.

## What's New in n8n 1.22.4 chart

This release marks an update to both the Helm chart (version 1.22.4) and the underlying application (version 2.26.6). Here are the key highlights:

- **Changed**: Updated the n8nio/n8n image version to 2.26.6
    - [Upstream Project](https://github.com/n8n-io/n8n)

To explore the complete list of changes, please visit the [release notes](https://github.com/community-charts/helm-charts/releases/tag/n8n-1.22.4) on GitHub.
